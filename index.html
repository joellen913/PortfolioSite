01

"SELF" mobile app

A Case Study on a Wellness App
High Point, NC

SUMMARY

In this case study, we'll explore the development and design of the "Self" mobile app, a comprehensive wellness and self-care platform designed to empower users to lead healthier lives. The app features a range of functionalities, including a Contacts page, a Yoga video section, a Home page for navigation, a page dedicated to Herbal Teas, a Virtual Journal page, and a Book section focused on meditation books.

Javascript code view Video Demo

APP FEATURES

HOME

Acts as the app's central hub, providing easy access to all features for a user-friendly experience

JOURNAL

Lets users record thoughts and emotions, fostering self-reflection and progress tracking and encourages self-reflection

TEAS

Informs users about health benefits of various herbal teas, promoting a natural approach to holistic wellness

VIDEOS

Provides guided sessions suitable for various skill levels to ensure inclusivity and engagement for all users

BOOKS

Recommends books for enhancing mental well-being through meditation, giving users selected books

UX/UI BENEFITS


Engaging Content:
The inclusion of Yoga videos, a Virtual Journal, and Herbal Teas keeps users engaged and ensures that they find valuable content relevant to their wellness journey.
Community Building:
The Contacts page fosters a sense of community and support, adding a social element to the app, which enhances user engagement and satisfaction.
Personalized Experience:
The app allows users to tailor their experience by choosing from various features, ensuring that "Self" remains relevant to individual needs.
Visual Appeal:
Aesthetically pleasing design, calming color schemes, and easy-to-read fonts create an attractive and soothing user interface, enhancing the overall user experience.

target audience

  • Young Professionals:
    Seeking a work-life balance and stress relief.
  • Wellness Enthusiasts:
    Interested in holistic health practices.
  • Novice Yoga Practitioners:
    Looking for guidance and video resources.
  • Meditation Enthusiasts:
    Eager to explore meditation literature.
  • Social Connectors:
    Those who appreciate building a supportive community.





02

"THRIFT TRACE" mobile app

A Case Study on a Thrifting App
High Point, NC

SUMMARY

Thrift Trace is a sustainable mobile app designed to promote thrifting and help users trace their carbon footprint. It features functionalities such as carbon footprint calculation based on thrifted clothes, a database for selling second-hand items, a menu page, profile management, and a section for viewing added items.

Javascript code view Video Demo

APP FEATURES

HOME

Acts as the app's central hub, providing easy access to all features for a user-friendly experience

Menu

Central hub for app navigation, providing easy access to all features

Carbon

Calculator:

Calculates users' carbon footprint based on the number of thrifted clothes

Seller Database

Allows users to list and sell second-hand items in a dedicated database

Profile

Enables users to enter and display personal details and add profile picture

UX/UI BENEFITS


Engaging and Intuitive Design:
A visually appealing and user-friendly interface enhances engagement.
Personalization:
Profile management and customization options provide a tailored experience.
Seamless Navigation:
Menu page ensures easy access to different app sections, improving overall usability.
Eco-conscious Messaging:
Clear communication about carbon footprint tracking reinforces sustainability goals.

target audience

  • Eco-conscious Consumers:
    Interested in reducing environmental impact.
  • Thrifting Enthusiasts:
    Look for a platform to sell and buy thrifted items.
  • Online Sellers:
    Seek a user-friendly platform for listing and managing items.
  • Users Interested in Sustainable Practices
    Want to incorporate eco-friendly habits into their daily lives.
