Logos

01

HPU MINDS

High Point University Club Rebranding
High Point, North Carolina

Creating the logo for "HPU MINDS," High Point University's innovative code club rebranding, was a collaborative effort that embodied the spirit of invention and education. The logo features a computer chip and a graduation cap, symbolizing the integration of technology and knowledge at the club. A contemporary, dynamic color palette was chosen to reflect the forward-thinking nature of the organization. This logo now represents "HPU MINDS," encapsulating its dedication to fostering innovation and collaboration, especially with industry luminary Steve Wozniak, in High Point, North Carolina, and beyond.

02

COLLEGE STICKERS SERIES

My Small Sticker Business
Germantown, MD

In March 2020, amid the COVID-19 outbreak, I launched "stickers by joellen" from my Germantown, Maryland home, creating stickers for colleges using just Adobe, a printer, a Cricut machine, and an Instagram account. In a span of 9 months, my business rapidly grew, accumulating 500+ sales. Notably, I collaborated with organizations like Temple University's drama department, High Point University Drama club, Phi Mu Sorority at James Madison University, and more. During the Black Lives Matter movement, I designed stickers supporting the cause, donating 50 percent of proceeds to the LDF, raising over $500. My efforts also led to a feature in a Juneteenth blog, available here.

01

PINK TAX INFOGRAPHIC

High Point University Club Rebranding
High Point, North Carolina

In my Adobe Illustrator-created infographic, I've harnessed my photography and editing skills to expose the "pink tax," a gender-based pricing inequality. Through visuals and concise content, I've defined, illustrated examples, emphasized economic consequences, provided a global context, and promoted change initiatives. This infographic serves as a compelling call to action, advocating for a more equitable marketplace.
